Solutions

:Collective innovations towards better communities

Code for good is a non profit platform that aspires to move communities towards innovating tech ideas and solutions that improve the quality of lives. We believe that innovative solutions are emerged from the collective experiences of communities, sensibilities of industry professionals and influence of sponsors. When these three pillars of society are brought together, unexpected and meaningful solutions will serendipitously emerge from the exchange of minds.
